About Us

CraftedByHeritage.com is the online platform created specially to empower women artisans with a low-cost, no-cost digital branding platform. We are committed to bridging the digital divide, making it possible for artisans to harness the full potential of their craft and connect with a global audience. 

Connect and expand

We're a close-knit community of women artisans who share a common passion for crafting excellence. Our community is a safe and welcoming space where you can freely share your experiences, knowledge, and dreams.

Got questions?

How do I know if your website is secure?

Look for the HTTPS:// prefix in the URL bar and the lock icon next to it. This means that the website uses Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption to protect your data. You should also avoid entering any personal or financial information on websites that you do not trust.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, as well as PayPal and Amazon Pay.

How long will it take for my order to arrive?

Most orders are shipped within 1-2 business days. Shipping times will vary depending on your location and the shipping method you choose.

Can I return or exchange my order?

Yes, we offer free returns and exchanges on all orders within 30 days of purchase. Simply contact us for a return shipping label.

What is your customer service policy?

We are committed to providing our customers with the best possible shopping experience. If you have any questions or problems with your order, please contact us and we will be happy to help.

How do I contact customer service?

You can contact customer service by email, phone, or live chat. Our customer service hours are Monday through Friday from 9am to 5pm EST.
